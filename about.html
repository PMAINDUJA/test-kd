About

Designer, visual problem-solver and color addict.

I’m Adeesha Induja, a Graphic, UI/UX and Visual Designer who loves building bold, modern visuals for brands and digital products. I mix strong typography, clean layouts and vibrant color to create work that looks good and feels easy to use.

My work lives in the space between strategy and style. I enjoy understanding what a brand or product needs to say, then translating that into visuals that feel sharp, consistent and memorable — whether it’s a logo, a mobile app screen or an entire social media launch.

Over time I’ve focused on building a design style that is colorful, modern and minimal. I like designs that are simple at first glance, but full of detail when you look closer: smooth spacing, clear hierarchy and little touches that make the experience feel polished.

  • • Working with startups, small businesses and personal brands.
  • • Comfortable designing for both web and mobile products.
  • • Always exploring new color palettes, typefaces and UI patterns.

How I like to work

I enjoy collaborating closely with clients and teams — asking questions, sharing early sketches and refining ideas together. A good project for me has clear goals, honest feedback and space to explore creative options before we lock anything in.

Whether it’s a full brand identity or a single landing page, my goal is simple: make it beautiful, functional and easy to remember.
